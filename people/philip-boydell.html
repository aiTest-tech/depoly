Philip Boydell

My Socials
LinkedIn

An Innovation Leader with a solid track record spanning three decades of commercialised products, Philip built and led ventures, assets and teams and pioneered long term market development in a multinational materials company. After recycling his first nylon resin in 1990, his career included significant roles in polymer recycle. Philip has a masters degree in chemistry from Oxford University and a PhD from what is today Université de Paris-Saclay. He has given invited lectures in the field of sustainability at the Ecoles Polytechniques of Lausanne and Paris.

This is what it is about

DePoly’s R&D department is responsible for developing sustainable processes to recycle all types of plastic, and ensuring the quality of monomers produced is of the highest quality. The team is a strong mixture of professional chemists, lab technicians, interns and quality experts, providing a dynamic and inspirational environment for innovation. Meet the team!
