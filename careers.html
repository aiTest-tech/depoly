Internship at Business Development Department

SHARE POSITION

Sion, Valais, Switzerland

Full time

SHOW MORE DETAILS

What we offer

3 to 6 months internship in a stimulating working environment, with the possibility of further opportunities at the end of the internship.

Being involved in the scaling up of new recycling technologies.

Making the first steps in the cleantech & sustainability industry.

Being part of an international team in the heart of the Swiss Alps.

Learning practical, on-the-job, business skills in the world of PET recycling.

Gaining experience in technology to help boost plastic circularity.

WHY WE ARE LOOKING

As an Intern at DePoly's Business Development Department, you will support the team in the go to market analysis & competitive intelligence, as well as Identify and structure Value Chain Ecosystem & Partnerships.

YOUR Responsibilities

Support in go to market analysis & competitive intelligence.

Identify and structure Value Chain Ecosystem & Partnerships.

Identify criteria & key decision factors to establish future plant locations.

Involvement and support in key business decisions / creation of business cases.

YOUR SKILLS

You are an ambitious student, currently enrolled in a Masters/ MBA program in Management, Technology, Business, or Engineering with first business experience.

Strong plus: Proven knowledge or a first experience in plastics production, recycling, waste management or circularity.

You are passionate about sustainability and understand the challenges of circular economy.

You think independently, are well organised and motivated, with the ability to cultivate and develop the role through the internship.

You can structure ambiguous problems and take action to solve them.

You can synthesise clear takeaways from complex information and provide recommendations using both qualitative and quantitative methods.

You can work effectively with diverse teams to come up with the best solution and move people and organisations to act.

You can communicate effectively with all audiences, including senior leaders, in a structured manner.

You are proficient in professional English (oral and written). Knowledge of French and / or German is a plus.

You have a Swiss working permit or are eligible to work in Switzerland.

‍

WHY WE ARE LOOKING

DePoly is a Swiss cleantech company, specializing in advanced recycling of PET and Polyester plastics. Recognized as 2024 Technology Pioneer by the World Economic Forum, and winner of the Top 100 Swiss Startup Award in 2024, we challenge the status quo and strive for excellence, with a shared passion for making an impact! We are looking for an Automation Engineer to help us continue working towards creating a sustainable circular economy for plastics!

YOUR Responsibilities

Designing, implementing, maintaining and improving control systems using PLC programs, robotics,

and sensor technologies.

Maintain and upgrade control systems for installation, integration, and operation.

Lead the Automation systems installation phase, testing, commissioning work and assist in start-up of systems including test plan, instrumentation configuration and set up validation, trouble shooting of electrical, and software Issues.

Understand safety procedures and Ensuring that electrical, instrument and control schemes operate within the framework of national and company safety standards.

Design and implement data communication protocols and implement best practices.

Preparation of punch list to ensure that the contractor rectifies all items before final commissioning and acceptance.

‍

YOUR SKILLS

BSc/BEng degree in Electrical Engineering or Mechanical engineering or similar fields.

5+ years of work experience in an industrial environment, or a solid foundation in the technology sector.

Hands on experience with industrial electrical and automation especially process control instrumentation.

Experience in commissioning and start-up of plants and production lines.

Experience in ATEX instrumentation is a plus.

Work to deadlines and adapt to changing priorities.

Ability to work cooperatively and supportively with a broad range of individuals with different levels of education and experience.

Be fluent in English and have an intermediate knowledge of French.

SHARE POSITION

Sion, Valais, Switzerland

Full time

SHOW MORE DETAILS

What we offer

A stimulating, diverse and dynamic working environment.

The chance for fast-track career growth with our company and make a real change.

Become involved in the scaling up and development of new recycling technologies.

‍

WHY WE ARE LOOKING

We are an award winning, Swiss company, that is changing the way we recycle plastic. Founded in 2020, we are a team of young, dynamic dedicated entrepreneurs who are committed to solving the plastic problem. Having built and optimised our pilot plant, we are in the process of construction of 500T per year showcase plant. We are also continually innovating, developing and optimising our DePoly process for PET recycling, but also advancing our techniques to recycle other consumer and industrial based plastic wastes. To assist in these ambitious aims, we are looking to expand our team by hiring an Electrical and Instrument Engineer.

YOUR Responsibilities

Designing, implementing, maintaining and improving electrical products and systems.

Preparing detailed E&I design deliverables e.g., circuit and schematic diagrams, control panel layouts, MCC layouts, cable routes etc.

Supervising the E&I project installation phase, testing, commissioning work and assist in start-up of systems including instrumentation configuration and set up validation, troubleshooting of electrical, and software Issues.

Ensuring that electrical, instrument and control schemes operate within the framework of national and company standards namely DSEAR andATEX legislation.

Preparation of punch list to ensure that the contractor rectifies all items before client final commissioning and client acceptance.

Providing technical assistance to maintenance instrument technician/electricians.

‍

YOUR SKILLS

BSc/BEng degree in Electrical Engineering or Technical Trade equivalent.

5+ years of work experience in an industrial environment, or a solid foundation in the technology sector. 

Hands on experience with industrial electrical and automation especially process control instrumentation.

Experience in commissioning and start-up of plants and production lines.

Experience in ATEX instrumentation is a plus. 

Work to deadlines and adapt to changing priorities.

Ability to work cooperatively and supportively with a broad range of individuals with different levels of education and experience.

Be fluent in English and have an intermediate knowledge of French.

Have a Swiss working permit or be eligible to work in Switzerland.

Plant Operator

SHARE POSITION

Sion, Valais, Switzerland

Monthey, Valais, Switzerland

Full time

SHOW MORE DETAILS

What we offer

A stimulating, diverse, and dynamic working environment.

The chance to grow and expand horizons with our company and make a real change.

Become involved in the scaling up and development of new recycling technologies.

WHY WE ARE LOOKING

We are an award-winning Swiss company that is changing the way we recycle plastic. Founded in 2020, we are a team of young, dynamic, dedicated entrepreneurs who are committed to solving the plastic problem. Having built and optimized our pre-demo plant, we are about to build and optimize our 500 T-per-year showcase plant. We are also continually innovating, developing, and optimizing our DePoly process for PET recycling, but also advancing our techniques to recycle other consumer and industrial-based plastic waste and implementing these processes to the pilot scale. To assist in these ambitious aims, we are looking to expand our team by hiring a Plant Operator.

YOUR Responsibilities

Operating the Showcase plant equipment, controlling and ensuring the smooth running of the process, and troubleshooting any issues.

Handling and processing of the large number of chemicals required for the running of the plant.

Close collaboration with Engineering and R&D teams in the development of new product tests and process optimization.

Writing reports and documents for process performance.

Organizing shipment of goods with the shipment responsible.

Organizing external and internal deliveries.

Regular check and updates of the inventory.

YOUR SKILLS

Chemical Operator/Technician.

Work experience in an industrial environment or a solid foundation in the technology sector.

Capable of independently running and operating various equipment and familiar with process control systems, a good foundation in chemistry and understanding of polymers is a plus.

Well organized and motivated, comfortable working in a fast-paced industrial environment, and good communication skills in small and large teams.

Have an intermediate knowledge of French and English.

Mastering Microsoft Office 365 (Excel and Word).

Mastering communication tools (Emails).

Familiar with the production chain (packaging, storage….).

Have a Swiss working permit or be eligible to work in Switzerland.

Knowledge of the Good Practices and Safety in chemical industries.

Is this a position for you? Send us your application!
